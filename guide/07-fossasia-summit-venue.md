# FOSSASIA Summit Venue

Address: True Digital Park, 101 Sukhumvit Road, Bangkok 10260, Thailand  
**BTS Station:** [BTS Punnawithi](https://maps.app.goo.gl/YVcPkiAATCsE4WPfA)  
**Skywalk**: Use **Exit 6** from BTS Punnawithi for a direct, covered walkway into the venue.  
Links to online map: [OpenStreetMap](https://osm.org/go/4S_Vnm9uN--) | [Google](https://maps.app.goo.gl/RPAjL8QaxsAAkgAFA)  
_**Note:** Bangkok traffic can be unpredictable. We strongly recommend traveling to the venue by BTS to avoid traffic congestion during peak hours._

## Catering

Food will be available in the exhibition area with vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. Additional restaurants and cafés, including halal options, are located throughout the conference building. Water dispensers are available—please help reduce waste by bringing your own water bottle.

## Nursing Mothers

A dedicated Mother’s Room is available. Please visit the **Registration Desk** to be directed to the area by a team member.

## Guests with Special Mobility Needs

The venue is equipped to assist guests with mobility issues. Please notify the organizers in advance at **office@fossasia.org** to ensure proper assistance.

## Parking

Free parking is provided at True Digital Park. However, using the BTS or bus is highly recommended during peak hours to avoid traffic.

## Lost and Found

All found items should be turned in at the **Registration Desk**; please check there if you have lost any belongings.
