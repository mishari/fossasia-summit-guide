# Pre-arrival and Visa

## Digital Arrival Card (TDAC)

Mandatory as of May 1, 2025. All visitors must complete the TDAC online at https://tdac.immigration.go.th/ within 72 hours before arrival and present the QR code to immigration.

## Visa Information

Many nationalities qualify for visa-free entry to Thailand for up to 60 days, while others may need a Visa on Arrival or a Tourist Visa. To find out the specific visa requirements based on your nationality, visit: https://www.thaievisa.go.th.

## Power

Thailand uses Type A, B, and C plugs (220V, 50Hz). [More details here](https://www.power-plugs-sockets.com/thailand/)
