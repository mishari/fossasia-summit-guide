# Payments (Cash, Card, QR)

Cash is widely used for everyday purchases, especially in markets, street food stalls, and taxis. Credit and debit cards (Visa/Mastercard) are accepted in more upscale establishments. Mobile Payments popular apps include LINE Pay, TrueMoney Wallet, and PromptPay, as well as QR code payments via banking apps. Check out this article for more information: https://wikitravel.org/en/Bangkok#Buy
